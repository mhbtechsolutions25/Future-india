About Future India

Who We Are

Future India Sports Club is one of Aurangabad’s leading sports and fitness destinations. With modern infrastructure and expert coaching, we provide a complete environment for athletes, beginners, and fitness enthusiasts to train, grow, and perform at their best.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote a healthy and active lifestyle by offering high-quality training, safe facilities, and a motivating atmosphere. We aim to build discipline, confidence, and strength through sports.

Our Facilities

We offer multiple sports under one roof including swimming, badminton, turf sports (cricket & football), gymnastics, martial arts, and a fully equipped modern gym. Our facilities are designed for all age groups and skill levels.

Why Choose Us

  • ✔ Safe environment for women
  • ✔ Separate swimming timings for women
  • ✔ Interactive and playful sessions for kids
  • ✔ Modern gym equipment
  • ✔ Clean and hygienic facilities
  • ✔ Experienced and supportive coaches
